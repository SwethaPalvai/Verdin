As business conditions and technology change, clients need the flexibility to rethink and revise their outsourcing strategies. Randstad Technologies works with clients to understand their internal challenges and then buildS ongoing outsourcing solutions for one or more of their technology functions.

Whether we deliver onsite or offsite, with our tools or our clients’ tools, we are accountable for the success and growth of the functions that we own through established service levels, trending analysis and effective communication. Randstad Technologies has worked with clients for decades to fulfill their outsourcing requirements by taking partial or full ownership of IT functions and delivering the high quality people, tools and processes tailored to help them attain their goals.

over 90% outsourcing client renewal rate

Our experience, commitment to delivery excellence and reliable infrastructure translate into a more than 90 percent outsourcing client renewal rate. Standard results and value from outsourcing engagements include:

  • Reduced labor and infrastructure costs
  • Enhanced end-user satisfaction
  • Increased efficiencies
  • Innovation that drives IT and business alignment

value proposition & expertise

  • We manage systems utilized by over 90 million end users
  • Our Service Desk operation supports over 1.8 million users, across 89 countries, handling over 4.5 million incidents annually
  • Over 1,200 employees dedicated to managed services engagements
  • Customer Satisfaction and Agent Quality scores average over 90 percent
